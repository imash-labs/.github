## imash

**Make your website make sense to AI.**

imash is a software studio that helps small and mid-sized businesses get cited by the answer engines people now ask instead of searching — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google's AI Overviews — and puts the same technology to work in their delivery workflows and products.

**What we do**
- **AI search readiness** — make your Drupal, WordPress, or custom site readable and citable by assistants
- **Workflow automation** — take the repetitive middle out of delivery: content ops, migrations, QA, internal tools
- **AI integration for startups** — scope, architect, and ship model-backed features

**How we work:** senior engineers end to end, fixed-scope and measured before and after, with no account layer or default retainer. Everything we build is documented and owned by your team.

Most of our work lives in client repositories — here we publish open-source tools and Drupal modules.
